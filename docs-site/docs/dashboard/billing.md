# Billing & Subscription

:::note PlexMCP Cloud only
Billing and subscriptions apply to PlexMCP Cloud (hosted). Self-hosted deployments have no usage limits or subscription fees.
:::

Manage your PlexMCP subscription, view usage, and update payment methods.

## Current Plan

View your current subscription:

1. Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. See your current plan and status
3. View usage for the current period

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Click **Subscribe**

Changes take effect immediately:
- New limits apply right away
- Prorated charge for the remainder of the period
- Next billing date remains the same

## Downgrading Your Plan

To downgrade:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Click **Confirm**

Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period:
- You keep current features until renewal
- No refund for remaining time
- Ensure you're within new plan limits

:::warning
Before downgrading, verify you meet the new plan's limits:
- MCP count
- Team member count
- API key count
:::

## Usage Alerts

Configure alerts for usage thresholds:

1. Go to **Billing** → **Alerts**
2. Set threshold (e.g., 80% of limit)
3. Choose notification method (email, webhook)
4. Save settings

You'll be notified when:
- Usage exceeds threshold
- You're projected to exceed limit
- You've reached your limit

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details
4. Click **Save**

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click the payment method
3. Click **Update**
4. Enter new details
5. Save

### Removing a Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click the payment method
3. Click **Remove**
4. Confirm removal

:::note
You must have at least one payment method on paid plans.
:::

## Billing History

View past invoices:

1. Go to **Billing** → **History**
2. See all past charges
3. Click any invoice to download PDF

Invoice details include:
- Invoice date and number
- Plan and period
- Amount charged
- Payment method used

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Choose your reason
4. Confirm cancellation

After cancellation:
- Access continues until period ends
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can resubscribe at any time

## Refunds

Our refund policy:

- **Within 7 days**: Full refund if requested
- **After 7 days**: Prorated credit for unused time
- **Annual plans**: Prorated refund available

Contact support@plexmcp.com for refund requests.

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed
- Verify card details are correct
- Check card hasn't expired
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact your bank if issues persist

### Wrong Amount Charged
- Check for prorated charges
- Review plan changes in history
- Contact support if unexplained

### Usage Discrepancy
- Check for failed requests (still counted)
- Review API key usage breakdown
- Contact support with details
